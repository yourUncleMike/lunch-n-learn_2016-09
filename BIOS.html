Adjust BIOS settings to boot thumb drive first.

But ... What if there's no hot-key to access the boot menu?

  • From the Windows login screen, click Restart
    with the SHIFT key held down!
  • At the Choose an option screen,
    choose Troubleshoot
  • At the Troubleshoot screen,
    choose Advanced options
  • At the Advanced options screen,
    choose UEFI Firmware Settings
  • At the UEFI Firmware Settings screen,
    choose [Restart]
