Adjust BIOS settings to boot thumb drive first.

But ... What if there's no hot-key to access the boot menu?

  • From login screen, click Restart
    with the SHIFT key held down!
  • At the Choose an option screen, choose
    Troubleshoot
  • At the Troubleshoot screen, choose
    Advanced options
  • At the Advanced options screen, choose
    Startup Settings
  • At the Startup Settings screen, choose
    [Restart]
