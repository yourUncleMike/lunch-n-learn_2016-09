Boot from DVD

  • Get LINUX FORMAT magazine #214.
    (Mint 18 edition)
  • Logout of Windows 10.
  • Insert Mint 18 DVD.
  • From login screen, click Restart
    with the SHIFT key held down!
  • At the Choose an option screen, choose
    Use a device
  • At the Use a device screen, choose
    Internal CD/DVD ROM Drive
  • At the Linux Format DVD 214 screen, choose
    Linux Mint 18 Cinnamon (64 bit), then
    Start Linux Mint 18 Cinnamon 64-bit
